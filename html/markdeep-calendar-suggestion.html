Possible syntax

Basically just autodetect a date followed by one of -, --, to, thru, through then another date: 
September 4, 2018 - September 6, 2018: Stuff
September 4, 2018 -- September 6, 2018: Stuff
September 4, 2018 to September 6, 2018: Stuff
September 4, 2018 thru September 6, 2018: Stuff
September 4, 2018 through September 6, 2018: Stuff
Easier for authoring if you can handle it, but understandable if not because it might make date parsing (more?) gross: 
September 4-6 2018: Stuff
September 4 thru 6 2018: Stuff
September 29 through October 3 2018: Stuff
September 29--October 3 2018: Stuff
